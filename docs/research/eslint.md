### Requirements from Parantion:
- Naming conventions:
    - Folders: snake_case
    - Vue components and classes: PascalCase
    - Proporties, methods: pascalCase
    - CSS: pascal-case

- Lenght of method: max 20 lines
- Props - with a required and a default value.
